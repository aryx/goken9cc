Those files comes from an old version of the Compcert project
https://github.com/AbsInt/CompCert and are now in the separate repo
https://github.com/AbsInt/CompCert-small-tests

Many of the C files actually come from The Computer Language Shootout Benchmarks
at http://shootout.alioth.debian.org/


Those files are useful to compare the speed of goken vs gcc (or clang) and
also to evaluate the usefulness of certain goken optimizations (-X vs nothing).
